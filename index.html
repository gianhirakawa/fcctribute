Gon Freecss of Hunter X Hunter Series

Gon is a young boy who's adventurous and free spirited one. A very innocent kid with lively personality. Once he sets his sight into something, he's very eager to achieve or obtain it.

Things to know about Gon Freecss:

  • His father, Ging Freecss is one of the top Hunters within the Hunter Association.
  • Grew up in Whale Island with his aunt Mito ever since he was born.
  • His adventurous spirit was first seen when he was able to catch Whale Island's legend.
  • Inspired by the stories of his hunter father, sets out on an adventure to become a hunter too.
  • Met friends that totally changed his life. Thought him companionship, endurance, hardship and perseverance.

As a hunter, the test won't be done unless you learn Nen. Things to know about Gon's Nen and his skills:

  • Gon is an enhancer type nen user.
  • As an enhancer, he's naturally born with a balanced nature. His strength, speed, reaction and reflexes are above average.
  • He also possess incredible eyesight, smell, taste and instinct.
